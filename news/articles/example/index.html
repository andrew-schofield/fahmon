This is an example article. The articles plugin provides a simple way for NanoBlogger to automatically generate and update articles for your weblog. Creating a new article is easy, create a new ".txt" file with the "--draft" option inside the articles directory. Then edit and save the file. To remove an article simply rename it's suffix to something other than "txt" or remove it from the articles directory. View the source of this article directly from ../example.txt.
